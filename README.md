**Goorac Learn** is the official learning platform provided by **Goorac Corporation**. This repository serves as the central hub for managing, developing, and maintaining our professional certificate course materials and platform infrastructure.

## 🚀 About Goorac Corporation

Goorac Corporation is a leading innovator in professional development and education technology. We are committed to bridging the gap between academic learning and industry demands through cutting-edge certification programs and practical skill-building initiatives.

Goorac Learn empowers professionals and students by providing high-quality, industry-relevant certification courses. Our platform focuses on practical skills, rigorous assessment, and recognized credentials to help learners advance in their careers.

## 🛠 Repository Structure

- `/courses` - Course content, syllabus documentation, and learning modules.
- `/assets` - Media files, branding elements, and course materials.
- `/src` - Platform source code and infrastructure scripts.
- `/docs` - Internal documentation, style guides, and operational procedures.
